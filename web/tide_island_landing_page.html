The island, in motion.

A code-built preview of the actual Tide Island states: clock, notification, player, control center with TLP modes, workspace overview, and status page.

Everything you check, folded into one island.

Tide Island keeps common desktop moments close without turning them into a full panel.

Music Player
Control MPRIS players with album art, progress, visualizer bars, and quick actions.
Control Center
Open a compact system panel for Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth, brightness, sound, battery status, and TLP power modes.
Workspaces
Preview Hyprland workspaces from a smooth top-center overview.
Lyrics
Show current lyrics directly in the island while music is playing.
Custom Page
Create a status strip with time, battery, CPU, RAM, volume, brightness, and more.
System OSD
Show volume, brightness, caps lock, workspace, and battery events cleanly.

Interactions

Simple gestures and shortcuts keep the island fast, readable, and out of the way.

Left clickOpen music player
Right clickOpen control center
Swipe leftShow lyrics
Swipe rightShow custom page
Super + TabOpen workspace overview
